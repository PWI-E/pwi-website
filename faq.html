Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about our aircraft LED lighting, coil windings, magnetometers, warranties, and contract engineering and manufacturing services.

Aviation LEDs

What makes our LEDs superior?

Our LED lights are backed by many FAA PMA and STC approvals. Each product utilizes superior components to make high-quality, reliable LED lighting. Made in the USA.

For more than 50 years, PWI has supplied OEM lights for Gulfstream, Dassault Falcon, Bombardier Challenger, Cessna Citation, Beechcraft King Air, Sabreliner and Learjet jets.

Benefits of upgrading to LEDs

  • Up to 100,000 hours of operation
  • LED-for-fluorescent light replacement installation
  • Eliminates ongoing maintenance
  • Install using the original aircraft wiring
  • FAA certifications
  • Less expensive than incandescent bulbs over the life of your aircraft
  • Custom lighting system designs are available

What are Lumens?

A lumen is a measure of light brightness. A higher lumens number means brighter light. Often business jet interior lighting is indirect, so there are no concerns about lights being “too bright” inside the aircraft cabin.

Special Missions aircraft such as AirMed are often fitted with custom-designed and brighter LEDs for patient care reasons. PWI often helps complete these specialized interior projects.

What is color temperature?

Color temperature choices allow aircraft owners to design the cabin look and feel.

Is interior aircraft lighting available without FAA certification?

Yes, completely custom aircraft cabin lights are available using an FAA Form 337 Field Approval, where required. Typically an FAA inspection (often performed by a DER) is required to verify fit, form, and function to complete the form and sign the aircraft logbook.

Coil Windings, Magnetometers & Warranties

Can you build my specific coil winding?

Coil windings started our business in 1963. We have experience creating a broad range of windings, usually used as a subassembly in a larger finished product. Custom magnetometers and fluxgates

PWI, Inc builds magnetometers using various wire gauges, core materials, and winding techniques to suit your specific needs. Call us to explore your magnetometer options.

There are only a few companies in the world that create fluxgate magnetometers by hand, and we are one of them. The engineering solutions offered by these magnetometers result in unique product designs that cannot be accomplished using other technologies.

Contract Engineering & Purchasing

What does Electrical Engineering mean?

Electrical engineering is a comprehensive range of scalable, value-added engineering services. Our experience performing engineering for a diverse group of customers can be applied to almost any project.

Often this work is outlined in a Statement of Work (SOW) proposal that clearly spells out the work process in detail, the desired features, a timeline and the estimated costs of the project.

We can provide as much engineering assistance for your product idea and requirements, as needed. Our services include anything from concept design development and prototyping, to in-house electronic testing and more. Our manufacturing personnel can also help build production units and kit them for shipment.

See the contract engineering page for more information.

What is contract manufacturing?

Contract manufacturing is an agreement between PWI and our customers to produce components and/or finished products once the design and engineering has been completed. We have customers that have completely engineered and tested their own prototypes, but need assistance in sourcing components and building a large quantity of units.

If you need help building your products or components — whether we completed the engineering or not — we can help. We have completed manufacturing projects in a variety of industries.

If we can assist you with any specific projects, just call or email.

See the contract manufacturing page for more information.

How can I purchase your products?

Aircraft Lighting: You can buy direct from us, or direct your favorite MRO to call us. You will most likely need an A&P at the MRO to perform the lighting installation and handle the paperwork/logbook. We have Authorized Distributors and Authorized Installation Center partners internationally.

Windings / Coils / Engineering / Production: These projects usually begin with a call or meeting to discuss the project in detail. We usually prepare a Statement of Work to outline the work to be performed along with an estimate of the time required to complete the project.

What is the PWI product return policy?

All warranty returns must be approved by PWI in advance of return shipment back to PWI. Shipments to PWI should have the PWI-assigned RMA number on the shipping box.

All customers must inspect products upon receipt. Products damaged during shipment should be addressed with the shipping provider. PWI does not assume any responsibility for damaged shipments. Any product returned for defects upon arrival must be returned, with an RMA number, within 30 days of the invoice for warranty consideration. There are no returns for products outside of warranty issues.
