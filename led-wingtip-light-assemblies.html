Overview

Add LED wingtips to your King Air or 1900 for added safety. STC-approved, with a 5-year warranty. Not a box of parts, it is delivered fully assembled, tested, and ready to install. Five times brighter than bulbs, with far less heat to protect wingtip lenses from warping, crazing, and discoloration. Lower current draw that reduces the electrical load on aircraft generators.

Why Upgrade

Five times brighter than original bulbs at a lower current draw. Far less heat protects wingtip lenses from warping and discoloration. No maintenance LEDs.

Installation

Delivered fully assembled, tested, and ready to install. Complete with new metal bracket and LEDs. Wiring harness included on King Air B300/350.
