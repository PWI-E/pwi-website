Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PWI, Inc. and its Precision Ground Intelligence (PGI / PGISR) division ("we," "us," or "our") collect, use, and protect personal information submitted through pgisr.com (the "Site"). By using the Site or submitting information through our contact form, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

PWI, Inc. is a US electronics manufacturer incorporated in Kansas, operating since 1963 at 109 South Knight Street, Wichita, Kansas 67213 USA. PGI (Precision Ground Intelligence) is the persistent ISR systems division of PWI, Inc. References to "PGI," "PGISR," "we," or "our" in this policy refer to PWI, Inc. and its PGI division collectively.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: sales@pgisr.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you complete the contact or demonstration-request form on this Site, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Company or organization name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Inquiry type (e.g., Live Demonstration, Government/Defense Sales, Dealer Inquiry)
  • Application or sector of interest
  • Message or mission-requirements text you choose to include

We do not collect payment card numbers, social security numbers, government identification numbers, or any export-controlled technical data through this Site. Do not submit classified information, controlled unclassified information (CUI), or ITAR-restricted technical data via this form.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Site, we and our analytics provider may automatically collect:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL
  • General geographic region (country/state level)

This information is collected through Google Analytics 4, a service provided by Google LLC. Google's data processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

2.3 Cookies

The Site uses cookies solely for Google Analytics. These are first-party analytics cookies that do not identify you personally and are not used for advertising or remarketing. You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time; the Site remains fully functional without them.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to:

  • Respond to your inquiry, demonstration request, or sales question
  • Route your inquiry to the appropriate internal team (e.g., Government & Defense Sales, Dealer & Reseller Programs, Technical Support)
  • Send follow-up communications directly related to your stated request
  • Maintain internal records of business communications
  • Comply with applicable laws and export regulations
  • Improve the Site's content and usability through aggregated analytics

We do not use your information for automated profiling, behavioral advertising, or any purpose unrelated to your inquiry.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within PWI, Inc.: Your inquiry may be shared with relevant personnel at PWI, Inc. or authorized PGI sales representatives to fulfill your request.
  • Service providers: We use Google Analytics for website analytics. No other third-party services receive your form submission data.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority, including in connection with export control enforcement by the US Department of State (Directorate of Defense Trade Controls) or Department of Commerce.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the same protections described here.

5. Export Control Notice

PWI, Inc. and PGI operate under the US International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR), Export Administration Regulations (EAR), and other applicable US export control laws. Information submitted through this Site may be subject to review under these laws. Do not submit technical data, specifications, drawings, or other information subject to export controls through this website. Inquiries involving ITAR-controlled systems or defense articles will be handled in accordance with applicable regulations.

6. Data Retention

We retain inquiry and contact records for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purpose for which they were submitted, to maintain business records, and to comply with legal obligations (including export control recordkeeping requirements, which may require retention for up to five years). Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' standard data-retention settings.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We are CMMC Level 1 certified and maintain cybersecurity controls aligned with NIST SP 800-171 for our internal systems.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of analytics tracking (by disabling cookies in your browser or using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sales@pgisr.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days.

9. California Residents

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the CPRA. We do not sell personal information. For California-specific requests, contact us at the address above and identify your request as a "CCPA Request."

10. Children's Privacy

This Site is intended for business and government professionals and is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Site after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
