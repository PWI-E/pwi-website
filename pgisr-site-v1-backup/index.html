How It Works

Deploy. Detect. Respond.

01

Deploy sensors at your perimeter

Choose from 9 sensor types — magnetometer, radar, FLIR, acoustic, seismic, and more. One enclosure can house multiple sensors. No power line required.

02

Sensors detect and classify threats

On-device AI filters out false positives and classifies events in real time — before data ever leaves the sensor. GPS pinpoints every alert automatically.

Alerts reach your team instantly

Encrypted data flows to your existing security platform via standard API. Your team gets actionable alerts — not noise — so response is fast and informed.
