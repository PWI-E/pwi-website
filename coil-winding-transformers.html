Coil Winding & Transformer Manufacturing

PWI has the ability to produce several different wire wound products and build them to your specific inductive component needs. We are able to use wire sizes ranging from 4 gauge to 51 gauge. PWI can create any product you need. Let our skilled production team build your next coil wound project.

Specifications

  • Wire Range4 – 51 gauge
  • Experience60+ years

How it works

1

Send us your specs

Send a drawing, electrical requirement, winding specification, sample, and production goals. Our team reviews the coil or transformer project and recommends next steps.

Helpful to include

  • Wire gauge & turns count
  • Core type & target inductance
  • Voltage, current & quantity
  • Production timeline
Send Winding Specs
2

We wind & test

Our production team winds and assembles each unit to your specification, then tests it against your requirements.

3

Your project, delivered

Completed coils and transformers ship ready for integration, backed by more than 60 years of winding experience.
