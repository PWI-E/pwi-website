The Origin

Not a sensor company. An intelligence company.

The security industry was full of hardware manufacturers. Almost none of them thought beyond the sensor. PGI was built to solve that — a system that begins at the perimeter and ends with a command-ready decision, with every step engineered and supported by the same team.

01 Mission

To deliver persistent, actionable ground intelligence to the operators and organizations that protect people, assets, and sovereignty.

02 Vision

A world where no perimeter goes unmonitored, no threat goes undetected, and no operator is left without the intelligence to act.

01 Capability Over Hardware

We measure success by what operators can do with our intelligence — not by sensor counts or hardware specs.

Intel-First Operator-Centric Full-Stack
