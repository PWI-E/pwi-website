Contract Manufacturing Solutions

PWI offers a full range of manufacturing services to meet any project requirements. Whether it is contract manufacturing, parts sourcing, volume production, kitting or engineering, PWI's highly skilled engineers and staff utilize their expertise to ensure all requirements are met.

How it works

1

Send us your project

Send any drawings, build requirements, part list, samples, and production goals. Our team will review your project, discuss options with you, and recommend next steps.

Helpful to include

  • Target quantity & timeline
  • Materials & testing needs
  • Packaging & documentation requirements
2

We review & build

We can build your parts to your specifications or take your concept and turn it into a finished product. We have experience in a wide variety of industries and a wide range of production volumes.

3

Your project, delivered

Let our field experience bring your next project to life.
